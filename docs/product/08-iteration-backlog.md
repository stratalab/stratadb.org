## What the reference teaches (and what it doesn't)

Neon's lesson is **craft density**: every claim on their page is backed by a *crafted
artifact* — a copy-on-write diagram, an autoscaling comparison chart, an interactive
activity timeline, a run-this-query block. Nothing is asserted with text alone. Their
atmosphere (noise, layered gradients, lighting) creates a sense of *place*.

What we deliberately do NOT import, per our own signed law: unverifiable big-number
theater (claims policy), testimonial/logo walls we haven't earned (no manufactured
proof), enterprise badge grids (not our stage), gradient-noise maximalism (02 §10 bans
stay). **Quiet competence stays; we raise craft, not volume.** The standing test: every
added artifact must still pass the squint test and depict something true.

## The gap, by dimension (ranked by leverage)

### I1 — Hero production value · the first five seconds
The fork concept is right; the execution is two plain panels. World-class versions of
the SAME idea: the terminals sit on a crafted stage (deliberate lighting, a more
beautiful branch arc, weightier split physics), the typing feels physical, the loop has
presence. This is where every visitor spends their first five seconds.

### I2 — Every claim gets a crafted artifact
- **"Forking is O(1) — nothing is copied"** deserves a *diagram* (refs vs copied pages —
  our copy-on-write story, drawn properly). Today it's body text.
- **The strata column** is a concept sketch of itself — layers should feel like material
  (depth, edge light, a real cross-section), not list rows with colored borders.
- **The graph primitive** begs for a small living visual (nodes settling) instead of a
  table row.
- **Time-travel strip**: sediment cells could look deposited, not card-shaped.

### I3 — Lighting & material system
Flat-and-hairline was the right reaction to the old site's mush, but *restrained
atmosphere* ≠ *no atmosphere*. One coherent light source: top-edge highlights on panels
(the shadow-float inset hints at it — make it systematic), screen-glow inside terminal
wells, a more crafted horizon (the current radial is a placeholder-grade glow). All
within the 02 §10 bans — this is lighting discipline, not effects.

### I4 — Iconography refinement
The five primitive icons shipped as one-pass drafts. A real pass: shared geometry,
optical balance, consistent stroke rhythm — they appear at every altitude from column to
(eventually) Foundry and Hub.

### I5 — Micro-interaction sweep
Copy chips and hover reveals exist; world-class is felt in the gaps — tab panel
transitions, focus choreography, the column layers' physicality on hover, scroll-linked
nav state. Budget-bounded by 03's caps.

### I6 — The live hero (R8 — the trump card)
When the wasm artifact lands, no reference site has this: the demo IS the product. Every
iteration above must keep the executor contract intact.

## Method

One dimension per iteration; each ends in a browser checkpoint against the reference AND
against our own law (squint test · claims audit · motion caps · budget gates). References
studied so far: neon.com. Candidates to study next: linear.app (restraint with depth),
vercel.com (typography), stripe.com/docs (docs craft).
